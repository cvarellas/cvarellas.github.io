editorial strategy

ProMazo

I conceptualized and created a detailed content marketing schedule for ProMazo, a two-year-old startup that crowd sources motivated college students to take on the tasks of companies like IBM, Fidelity Investments and Whirlpool in on-campus internships during the school year. Visit our Medium page, and read my most popular content marketing piece.

North by Northwestern

I brainstormed, assigned, guided and published over 40 stories over the course of one academic quarter as NBN's entertainment editor, curating content that would pique students' interest, deliver accurate information and maintain NBN's signature "tone" (colloquial, yet knowledgeable). As managing editor, I oversaw the activity of the Politics and Science & Tech sections, participating in executive decisions and mentoring section editors in shaping their content schedules. Read about the time I arranged an interview with comedian Adam DeVine, or the time I channeled One Direction in quitting a student group.

Northwestern Art Review

I created an online editorial content schedule to accompany one of the most widely read undergraduate art journals in the country, publishing approximately two stories a week on a website I redesigned. I organized a series called Artists of Northwestern, featuring some of Northwestern's most promising painters, photographers and musicians.
